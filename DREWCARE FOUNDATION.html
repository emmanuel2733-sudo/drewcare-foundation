Who we are

A cheerful support system for children who need extra care.

DrewCare Foundation exists for children whose learning, speech, movement, sensory, or developmental needs require patient attention. We work with families, schools, therapists, and volunteers so children can grow with dignity and confidence.

  • Early intervention
  • Family guidance
  • Inclusive education
  • Child-centered therapy

What We Do

These services are drawn from the DrewCare brief: practical care pathways that help each child receive the right support at home and in school.

A

Special Needs Assessment

We observe strengths, challenges, learning patterns, communication needs, and family goals before building a support plan.

S

Speech Therapy

Children receive guided support for speech, language, listening, social communication, and confidence in expression.

P

Physiotherapy

Movement, balance, posture, coordination, and daily functional activities are supported through child-friendly sessions.

C

Parent & Caregiver Training

Families learn routines, communication tools, home exercises, behavior support, and ways to celebrate steady progress.

I

School Support & Inclusion Programs

We help schools adapt classrooms, support teachers, reduce stigma, and create learning environments where children belong.

Our approach

Care plans that connect therapy, home, and school.

Children make stronger progress when everyone around them understands the same plan. DrewCare brings parents, caregivers, therapists, and educators into one supportive circle.
